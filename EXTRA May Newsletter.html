Sidewalk Repairs

The sidewalk repairs within Eagleton have now been completed.

Several concrete panels were replaced, some uneven spots were ground down and railings were installed at three locations to comply with ADA (Americans with Disability Act) requirements.

Should you have any questions or concerns, please contact the Property Manager’s Office at (561) 625-8588.

Eagleton Landscaping

The Championship POA's landscape team, made up of Dianne Silich and Paul Slepakoff, is working hard to ensure that the community looks its best at all times - not just for you, our homeowners, but also for your friends and guests.

They meet on a regular basis with representatives of Revival Landscaping, our contracted landscape company, and have created a prioritized list of items and areas that need to be updated or improved.

If you have any questions, suggestions or concerns about landscaping, please contact the Property Manager’s Office at (561) 625-8588.

OUR POA WEBSITE

The Championship POA launched its website in June of 2021 with the intention of keeping all of the homeowners within Eagleton up to date and aware of what is taking place, and will be taking place, within our community.

On the site you can find information about what the Championship POA is, a listing of all the members of the Board of Directors, some information about staying safe in Eagleton, a calendar of meeting for both the PGA POA and our own POA, a link to prior newsletters, and an area where you can also post questions for the Board (or a least the Editor) to answer.

Tucked up in the upper right of the screen is a link named "Boards/Boards of Governors" and on that link you can find a listing of all the Directors, not only of the Champ POA but also of the various Eagleton HOA Board members as well as the PGA POA Board of Governors.
