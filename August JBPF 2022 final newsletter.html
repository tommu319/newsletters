Enjoy a SAFE holiday!

On Monday, July 4th, the Town of Juno Beach will be closed for the national holiday commemorating the Declaration of Independence.

Although the Town Center will be closed, our dedicated Police Officers will be on duty working to help keep our Town safe... but they need your help.

Despite the dangers of fireworks, few people understand the associated risks; devastating burns, injuries, property damages/fires, and even death.

Fitness Center Ribbon Cutting

On June 30th, Mayor DD Halpern along with Chief of Police Brian Smith, Police Chaplain Emily McGee and Town Councilman Alex Cooke officiated over the ribbon cutting of the Town's new Employee Fitness Center.

Rob Thomson, owner of Waterfront Properties and his brother Steve Thomson, President of Steve Thomson Homes provided the funding for the new facility and were presented with plaques to show the Town's appreciation for their generosity.

The new center is equipped with state of the art training equipment, new lighting, and special flooring that makes it a modern, up to date facility where our Juno Beach Police Officers train on an almost daily basis.

A plaque commemorating the Thomson brothers donation was also affixed to the wall in the Fitness Center.

The Juno Beach Police Foundation provided additional funding for the latest in audio visual and sound equipment for the facility.

We are grateful of your support!

Given the challenges brought on by the pandemic, we are more attuned than ever to the things people do, big and small, that make our lives better.

We are especially grateful to you for supporting the Juno Beach Police Foundation. Despite the difficulties of the past two years, we have achieved a number of important successes in support of our mission to advance the cause of public safety in Juno Beach.

We continued to strengthen ties between police officers and residents by bringing police and community members together.

We also continued our successful programs procuring equipment, training, and technology for the Juno Beach Police Department, and as a supporter of the new Town fitness center, we worked to help keep our Officers fit.

Juno Beach is a thriving community, with a diverse set of wonderful homeowners. Our Foundation Board has embraced the goal of keeping our Town and our residents safe, while at the same time maintaining and improving the Quality of Life in Juno Beach.

Accordingly, we have ambitious plans for the coming months, including supporting initiatives to promote officer health and wellness, making in-service training more readily available and continuing to provide new and additional equipment and technology initiatives.

On behalf of our dedicated, all-volunteer Board, and the sworn and non-sworn members of the JBPD, we extend our sincere gratitude for your investment in the Juno Beach Police Foundation and pledge to continue our efforts to support the cause of public safety in our growing and thriving community.
