POLICE AWARDS CEREMONY - WEDNESDAY

The Juno Beach Police Department proudly announces that it will hold its Annual Awards Ceremony on Wednesday, May 25th, immediately preceding the Town Council Meeting. The Ceremony will take place in Council Chambers at 4:30 PM

The following individuals will be recognized with the these awards::

  • Police Officer of the Year - Officer Gregory Mullen
  • Supervisor of the Year - Detective Jose Nuñez
  • Civilian Employee of the Year - Alyssa Grammatico
  • Volunteer of the Year - Alan Patti
  • Community Partnership Award - Brendan Corrigan

In announcing the ceremony Chief of Police Brian Smith said, "this is always one of my favorite times of the year as we get to reflect on situations that are truly life changing, that matter, and reflect on just a small portion of the splendid work the men and women of the Juno Beach Police Department do on a daily basis".

The Chief will present the awards along with the Town Council and he encourage all residents of the Town of Juno Beach to attend and help congratulate this year's award recipients.

Police Foundation Annual Campaign

The Annual Campaign of the Juno Beach Police Foundation began in December 2021 and continues well into 2022.

Incorporated in 2018, the Police Foundation has, with the support of the community, purchased numerous important items for the Juno Beach Police Department to help improve both their efficiency and community safety.

Grants from the Foundation to the Juno Beach Police Department well exceed $250,000.

With your support and that of the overall Juno Beach community the Juno Beach Police Foundation will continue in its efforts to "Help Ensure the Safety of Juno Beach." Thank You!
